Knowledge Base & FAQ

Find quick answers to common questions. Start by searching or browse the categories below.

How do I check the status of my support ticket?
After submitting a ticket, you will receive a confirmation email with a ticket ID and a link to track its status. You can also log in to your client portal to view all your active and past tickets.
What are your official support hours?
Our standard support hours for tickets and live chat are Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (PST). Phone support for urgent and critical issues is available 24/7.
What information should I include in my support request?
To help us resolve your issue faster, please include your product/service name, a detailed description of the problem, any error messages you've received, and steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots are also very helpful.
Do you offer service-level agreements (SLAs)?
Yes, we offer custom SLAs for our enterprise clients and managed service partners. These agreements guarantee response times and resolution targets based on issue priority. Please contact our sales team for more information.
